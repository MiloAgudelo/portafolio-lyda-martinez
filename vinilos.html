Vinyl

This project is a collection of three vinyls inspired by the Argentine band "Los Fabulosos Cadillacs". Its concept arises from the musical and narrative essence of the group, which has captured cultural syncretism in its lyrics and sounds.

From their music, three albums are developed: Bocha, Rioba and Yeca, each one representing a space where this fusion comes to life.

Bocha evokes the road trip, where landscapes, music and stories intertwine.

Rioba reflects the identity of the neighborhoods, with its mix of customs and community.

Yeca captures the celebration in the streets, between party, protest and urban culture.
