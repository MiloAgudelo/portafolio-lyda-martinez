Tarot

Get Tangled or Untangled! It is a tarot that is born from the connection between spirituality and introspection. Through the metaphor of knots, it represents how our lives are intertwined with others, how our decisions can entangle us or free us, and how each experience is a thread in the fabric of our existence.

Each tarot card is constructed from photographs of knots. This tarot invites us to reflect on our own path, offering a visual and symbolic tool to understand our experiences.
